Baked Chicken with Asparagus & Zuchini

  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon
  • Chicken thigh
  • Garlic
  • Organo
  • Asparagus
  • Zuchini
  1. Combine Olive oil, lemon juice, garlic and oregano, whisk until combined well. Add chicken thighs
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Sear chicken until the skin is crispy
  3. Place the chicken, asparagus, zuchini and lemon slices on a sheet pan. Pour over any drippings from the searing pan.
  4. Bake until chicken is cooked through.
