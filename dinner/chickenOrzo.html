Chicken and Orzo

  • 1.5 pounds of chicken
  • 1 Tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. cround cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed chili flakes
  • 5 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 4 Cups chicken broth
  • 12 oz Orzo
  • 1/4 cup fine chopped fresh dill
  • 1/2 english cucumber
  • 2 cups cherry/grape tomato
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta
  • 1 Tbsp lemon Juice
  • Salt & fresh cracked pepper
  1. In a large bowl, toss chicken, coriander, paprika, red pepper, cinnamon, 3/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper
  2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a high sided skillet over mediaum heat. Sear chicken on all sides, dont crowd the pan. Sear in batches if need be. Cook chicken until cooked through.
  3. Remove chicken and add a small splash of olive oil. Add garlic and stir until fragrant. Stir in broth, orzo & 1/2 tsp salt and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until orzo is tender and most of the broth has been absorbed. ~12-14 minutes
  4. Remove from heat. Stir in dill and the chicken.
  5. In a medium bowl, toss diced cucumber, tomato, feta, lemon juice, remianing Tbsp of olive oil and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spoon cucumber salad over chicken and orzo. Sprinkle on more dill.
