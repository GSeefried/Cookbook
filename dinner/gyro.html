Gyro

  • 1 Small yellow onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp. fresh oregano or 2 tsp dried
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • 2 lb protien of your choice
  • 2 cups whole milk greek yogurt
  • 1 english cucumber
  • 2 Tbsp. chopped dill
  • flat bread or pita
  • sliced tomato
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Thinly sliced red onion
  1. Mince garli and combine with oregano, salt & pepper. Rub on your protien.
  2. If using a ground meat, mix spices into the ground meat and form in a loaf pan. Bake at 350 until protien is cooked through.
  3. If using chunks or filets of meat, rub spices on meat and pan cook or bake until protien is cooked through
  4. While protien is cooking, in a medium bowl stir together yogurt, grated or finely minced cucumber, 3 cloves of minced garlic, dill and 1 teaspoon salt. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  5. For crispier edges, slice protien and place under the broiler for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Warm pita or flatbreads and assemble with protien, Tzatziki sauce and sliced veggies.
